Ways to Give

Making a donation to the West Side Alumni Foundation, Inc. is easy and secure. Gifts can be made through credit/debit card transactions, personal checks, cashier’s checks, money orders (made payable to West Side Alumni Foundation, Inc.), corporate, or matching gifts. Recurring gifts can be made through regular and consistent contributions to WSAF. You can also set up your credit card to be charged a certain amount or by scheduling a deduction from your checking account at a frequency of your choosing.

  • Online - Credit Card or PayPal
    Use our simple and secure online form to make a credit card or PayPal gift that can quickly go to work to support the WSAF and its initiatives.
  • By Mail – Check or Money Order
    If you prefer to mail your gift, send your check or money order made payable to:
    West Side Alumni Foundation, Incorporated
    P.O. Box 9292
    Highland, IN 46322
    Upon receipt of your gift, you will be sent a receipt confirming it. Please keep your receipt for tax purposes.
  • Corporate gift
    Socially-aware companies recognize the connection between the success of their community and the success of their business. We invite corporations to support WSAF through cash donations or in-kind contributions such as donated goods or services. When making your corporate contribution, please specify the amount, as well as the type, of donation.
  • Matching gift
    Many employers sponsor matching gift programs that will match the charitable contributions made by their employees. If your employer has a matching gift program, the amount of your contribution to WSAF could be doubled or tripled. You will be recognized for the full amount of your own gift plus your employer’s matching gift. These gifts also allow WSAF to successfully pursue its mission of providing funding, support and lasting solutions for the students of West Side Leadership Academy.
  • Fundraising
    West Side alumni, family, faculty, staff, and friends are encouraged to assist the WSAF in raising funds to benefit the academic, athletic, and social experiences of West Side Leadership Academy students. If you are interested in sponsoring a fundraising event for the WSAF, please contact the WSAF for more information at info@westsidealumnifoundation.org
